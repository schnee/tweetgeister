Tweetgeister!

Tweetgeister was a little experiment performed by Brent Schneeman in 2010 - 2011. It attempted to use Natural Language Processing to cluster tweets that were already associated (by a hashtag or by a Twitter search). Tools used were Lingpipe and protovis.js and is stored at github. These days, I'd probably use R and D3.js.
